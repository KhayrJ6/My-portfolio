My top HHK Case Study!

Customer Engagement & PR

Company: HHK

The catering e-commerce company focuses on delivering high-quality meals while indextaining fast order processing and exceptional customer service. The business values operational efficiency, clear data visibility, and the ability to understand customer patterns to improve sales and repeat purchases.

Goal:Enhancing Order Intelligence, Revenue Tracking and Marketing Performance Through Data
The What

My responsibility was to strengthen the company’s data visibility and uncover insights that could guide sales, inventory management, and marketing decisions. The company relied heavily on manual tracking, making it difficult to understand revenue patterns, high-performing products, and the impact of social media efforts on customer orders.

The How
  1. I designed a real-time sales and social media performance dashboard in Excel that consolidated daily orders, product performance and customer engagement data, allowing us to identify high-converting posts and optimal posting windows.
  2. I created a structured order-to-customer logging system using Excel formulas, VLOOKUPs, pivot tables and clean data validation rules to eliminate manual errors and improve weekly revenue accuracy.
  3. I deployed a lightweight AI insights prototype using Python, Pandas and the Gemini API to generate weekly content and marketing recommendations that supported faster decision-making.
  4. I analyzed product demand trends and customer order frequency to highlight top-selling meals, periods of peak activity and opportunities to adjust pricing or bundle offerings.
The Impact
  • Order volume increased significantly as the business began posting content at the highest-performing engagement times.
  • Weekly revenue tracking accuracy improved, giving the founder confidence in financial decisions and inventory planning.
  • Campaign decision-making became faster and more data-driven due to weekly automated insights.
  • The team gained a deeper understanding of customer behaviour, enabling better product prioritization and more targeted marketing.
