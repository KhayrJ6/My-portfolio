Audience insights and Strategy

Company: Balad Shelter

Balad Shelter is a growing real estate startup focused on connecting clients to verified properties while providing transparent pricing, responsive communication, and data-driven recommendations. The company values clarity, trust, and customer experience, aiming to make property discovery simpler and more efficient.

Goal:Building a Data Foundation for Property Performance, Client Insight and Sustainable Growth
The What

My responsibility was to establish strong data practices that could help the founder understand customer behaviour, listing performance and revenue patterns. Balad Shelter was still early stage with limited traffic and no formal analytics structure, so the goal was to create clarity from the little data available and use it to guide strategic decisions on listings, pricing and client engagement.

The How

I began by building the company’s first data tracking framework using Excel, Google Sheets and SQL. This allowed us to capture and organize information on property listings, inquiries, locations, client interest types and pricing changes. I complemented this structure with Python and Pandas to run small but meaningful analyses on inquiry patterns, high-demand property categories and listing performance across neighbourhoods.
I also created a clean and interactive dashboard that displayed weekly trends in inquiries, availability and price movements. The dashboard became a visual decision tool that helped the founder quickly see what was working and where opportunities were emerging. Through continuous monitoring, I identified the strongest performing listing segments, highlighted locations with rising interest, and surfaced the early metrics that mattered most for growth.

  • Market Positioning
The Impact

The company gained its first reliable view of how prospects interacted with different property types. This insight led to more intentional listing selections, a clearer pricing strategy and improved follow-up with potential clients. Lead response time improved as the founder began prioritizing inquiries based on data rather than assumptions, and we recorded meaningful improvements in inquiry quality and overall conversion. The structured data foundation positioned Balad Shelter to operate less on guesswork and more on evidence, supporting stronger decision-making and setting up the startup for more sustainable growth.
