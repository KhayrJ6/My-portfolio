My top Plexus Case Study!

Customer Engagement & PR

Company: Plexus

Plexus is an emerging SaaS and IT solutions startup committed to helping businesses operate more efficiently through simple, scalable technology. The company values clarity, speed of execution, and the ability to use data to guide product improvements, customer engagement, and long-term growth

Goal:Building the Early Analytics Backbone to Guide Product Adoption and Revenue Growth

Sample data slide

The What

My responsibility was to help Plexus understand how users interacted with the platform, what was driving sign-ups, and where potential revenue opportunities were emerging. With limited early-stage data and no existing reporting structure, the goal was to build a reliable foundation for tracking core metrics and guiding decisions that could support sustainable product and revenue growth.

The How
  1. I defined and tracked the company’s first set of essential SaaS metrics, including sign-ups, activation rate, retention patterns and early revenue indicators, using SQL and structured spreadsheets.
  2. I analyzed onboarding and usage behaviour to identify the points where users dropped off, helping the team refine flows that improved product experience.
  3. I conducted channel performance reviews across organic, referral and small paid campaigns to highlight where the most cost-efficient traffic and user quality came from.
  4. I built growth insight reports that linked product usage data to potential revenue paths, helping the founders prioritize features and customer segments with the highest long-term value..
The Impact
  • The company gained a clear understanding of which acquisition channels were worth reinvesting in based on user quality and engagement.
  • User activation improved as the onboarding experience was refined using insights from behaviour analysis.
  • Plexus established early clarity on revenue opportunities and growth levers, creating a stronger strategic direction for the next phase of expansion.
